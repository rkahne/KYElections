Kentucky Election 2015: Matt Bevin and the Medicaid Expansion

The graphic above shows the relationship between the vote for Matt Bevin and Jenean Hampton in 2015 and the Per Capita Medicaid Expansion. Those with a high proportion of votes for Matt Bevin and a large increase in Medicaid population are the counties in the upper right corner. These individuals could be seen to be voting against their own interests.

Update 11/7/2015
